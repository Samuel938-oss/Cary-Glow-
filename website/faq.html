Preparation & Aftercare

For Botox and fillers: avoid blood thinners (aspirin, ibuprofen, fish oil) for 3 days before. For laser treatments: avoid sun exposure and shave the area 24 hours before. We'll send you specific prep instructions after booking.

Treatments & Results

Botox results typically last 3–4 months. Many of our clients come in 3–4 times per year to maintain their results. After multiple treatments, some clients find their results last even longer as the muscles learn to relax.

Most of our treatments have little to no downtime. Botox and fillers have zero downtime — you can return to work immediately. Microneedling and chemical peels may cause mild redness for 24–48 hours.

Most clients describe Botox as a tiny pinch — very manageable. For fillers, we apply a topical numbing cream beforehand. Comfort is our priority and we'll always check in with you throughout the treatment.

Absolutely. Many clients combine Botox with a HydraFacial in the same appointment. We'll plan your treatment schedule during your consultation to get you the best results safely and efficiently.

All injectable treatments are performed by Dr. Sofia Reyes, a board-certified physician with over 8 years of aesthetic medicine experience. Other treatments are performed by our licensed aestheticians.
