Her Story

"I wanted to create the kind of practice where I'd send my own family."

Dr. Reyes completed her medical degree and spent years in clinical medicine before finding her passion in aesthetic medicine. She trained under some of the top injectors in the country, earning advanced certifications in facial anatomy and cosmetic injectables.

When she opened Cary Glow, she made a decision that sets her apart from most med spas: every injectable treatment would be performed by her personally — not delegated to a nurse or aesthetician. That level of hands-on care is rare, and her clients feel it.

"The aesthetic medicine industry can feel very transactional. I built Cary Glow to be the opposite — a place where you feel known, where your goals matter, and where the results are something you're genuinely proud of."
